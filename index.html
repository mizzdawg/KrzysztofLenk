KRZYSZTOF LENK

Krzysztof (Kris) Lenk, 1936–2018, a renowned graphic designer, information architect, and consultant in visual logic. Professor at Łódź Academy of Fine Arts and Design in Poland (1973–1982), and Rhode Island School of Design in the USA (1982–2010). Co-founder and creative director of studio Dynamic Diagrams (1990–2001).

“Good typography is not rooted in the technology of composition or printing, but in the complicated processes of human perception.”

–Krzysztof Lenk
